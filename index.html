Why This Plan
Porto gives you the best of both worlds. You spend the 4th of July weekend in one of Europe's most romantic and walkable cities — port wine at sunset on the Dom Luís Bridge, the Ribeira waterfront glowing at night, pastéis de nata for breakfast. It's only two nights away, so you're back in Kaiserslautern by Monday evening. Then the castle and Munich block on Tuesday and Wednesday delivers the two must-do's without rushing. That leaves you with four full chill days at home — more than any other plan that includes a real international trip. No 5-hour drives to Amsterdam. No bouncing between three countries in four days. Just a clean, compact trip abroad, the Bavarian highlights, and plenty of time to cook dinner together, walk the Pfalzerwald, and actually enjoy being in the same place.

The Flow
Fri Jul 3 — Melissa lands at Frankfurt, drive to KL, Biergarten dinner, early night.

Sat Jul 4 — Drive to Luxembourg Airport (1h), fly Ryanair FR2863 at 2:55 PM. Arrive Porto 4:30 PM. Walk Ribeira, riverside dinner, port wine on Dom Luís Bridge at sunset. 4th of July in Portugal.

Sun Jul 5 — Porto morning: Mercado do Bolhão, Clérigos Tower, Livraria Lello, Palácio da Bolsa + Igreja de São Francisco. Afternoon train from São Bento to Viana do Castelo — funicular to Santa Luzia for panoramic views of the Lima Valley. Back for sunset + francesinha dinner.

Mon Jul 6 — Heritage Tram 1 along the Douro to Foz do Douro beach, fly Ryanair FR2862 at 1:10 PM. Land Luxembourg 4:35 PM, home by 6 PM.

Tue Jul 7 — Tesla road trip to Neuschwanstein (258 km, Supercharger stop at Merklingen). Shuttle bus up to Marienbrücke for the iconic photo, 1:40 PM castle tour, then drive to Munich. 2 nights in Munich.

Wed Jul 8 — Full Munich day. Marienplatz, Viktualienmarkt, Englischer Garten, Residenz, evening walk along the Isar.

Thu Jul 9 — Munich morning (Nymphenburg or shopping), drive back to KL.

Fri Jul 10 — Chill day. Sleep in, coffee, Pfalzerwald walk, cook dinner together.

Sat Jul 11 — Farewell day. Saturday Wochenmarkt, revisit favorites, farewell dinner.

Sun Jul 12 — Drive to Frankfurt, 9:45 AM flight home.
